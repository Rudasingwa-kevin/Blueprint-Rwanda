Help Center

Common questions about booking, payments, and our services.

How do I book an accommodation or car?
Simply browse the listings on our "Stay" or "Rent a Car" pages. Once you find something you like, select your dates and follow the guided booking process to confirm your reservation.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major credit cards, Mobile Money (MTN & Airtel), and secure bank transfers. You can also choose to pay at check-in for select properties.
Is there a cancellation policy?
Yes, each listing has its own cancellation policy clearly stated on the details page. Generally, most bookings offer free cancellation up to 48 hours before the start date.
How can I become a partner?
Head over to our "Partners" page or sign up for an "Owner" account from the verification screen to start listing your own properties and services.
Is transport between cities provided?
Yes, we offer professional car rental services and guided tours that include inter-city transport. You can browse these options in the "Logistics" section of our services.
